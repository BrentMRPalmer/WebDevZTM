The Internet Backbone

What do the arrows mean? (Web page -> Google servers)

1) Home Wifi provided by router
2) Router is connected to a modem
3) Modem transmits to signal towers
4) Signal towers connect to ISP network
5) ISP connected to "Internet backbone"

What is the Internet Backbone?
Look up the Submarine Cable Map
It shows where all of the cables are that provide the Internet
Those cables make up the Internet Backbone
