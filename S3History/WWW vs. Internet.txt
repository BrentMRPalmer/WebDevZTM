WWW vs. Internet

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the World Wide Web in 1989.

Started with ARPANET in 1969, a military project.
It kept growing
Academics needed a way to share documents.

The world wide web is a common language that computers can
speak (before it was as if there was a language barrier
between languages that made it difficult to share documents).

Tim created the first browser and the first server.
